Kikagati Hydropower Plant Project – Uganda

+Axis Surveys provided specialized surveying and drone mapping services for the Kikagati Hydropower Plant, a cross-border renewable energy project between Uganda and Tanzania. Once complete, the plant will supply clean, sustainable electricity to thousands of households across East Africa.

Project Overview

The Kikagati Hydropower Plant faced challenges due to its cross-border location on the Kagera River and difficult terrain. Our team delivered precise geospatial data to support engineering design, planning, and regulatory compliance, ensuring smooth execution of this vital renewable energy initiative.

Key Facts

Location

Kagera River, Uganda–Tanzania border

Capacity

16 MW Hydropower Plant

Beneficiaries

Over 40,000 households

Technology

Drone Survey & GIS Mapping

Scope of Work

  • Drone-based aerial survey and mapping
  • Topographical and geospatial data collection
  • Boundary demarcation for site planning
  • Preparation of digital terrain models and survey reports
  • Collaboration with civil engineers for design integration

Technologies & Tools

GIS Mapping
UAV / Drone Surveys
AutoCAD & Civil 3D
Data Analytics

Project Timeline

  • Phase 1: Preliminary drone survey & feasibility studies
  • Phase 2: Boundary mapping & site planning
  • Phase 3: Terrain modeling & digital mapping
  • Phase 4: Ongoing monitoring & progress reporting
