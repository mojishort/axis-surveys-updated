Who We Are

+Axis Surveys is a premier land surveying and consultancy firm based in Kenya, dedicated to delivering top-tier solutions in all matters related to land. With over three years(3+) of experience both within and outside Kenya, +Axis Surveys has built a reputation for excellence,professionalism, and reliability. Utilizing the latest in surveying technology and advanced data analysis techniques, we ensure precision, efficiency, and accuracy in every project. Our modern approach to land surveying allows us to offer unmatched services across a wide range of land-related processes.
