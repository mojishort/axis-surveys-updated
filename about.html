About +Axis Surveys

+Axis Surveys is a premier land surveying and consultancy firm based in Kenya, dedicated to delivering top-tier solutions in all matters related to land. With over three years of experience both within and outside Kenya, we’ve built a reputation for professionalism, precision, and dependability.

Utilizing the latest surveying technology and advanced data analysis techniques, we ensure precision and efficiency in every project we handle.
